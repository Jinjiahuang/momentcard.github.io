About momentcard

Step 1: Take a picture of the moment you feel like sharing.

Step 2: Add filters, stickers and some emojis. Send us one line description of who this is for, what is the moment and their address.

Step 3: We’ll take it from here. Wait to hear from your excited friend within the next day!

Choose the exact moment you captured

Our service allows you to capture those little moments that are hard to describe in words. You can send your loved ones a postcard with a picture clicked by YOU, customize it with stickers and filters and share it in the fastest way possible. By sending a postcard, we also help preserve that human touch as a physical artifact which can be stored in a story to tell and a memory that won’t be forgotten.

Send a handwritten postcard signed by you

An additional feature we can offer is adding your own custom signature to the card. You can either attach a photo of your signature and we’ll make sure to put it on or you can electronically send us a little signature to use. If you’d like, we’ll even make sure that the note is handwritten in a beautiful calligraphic style.
